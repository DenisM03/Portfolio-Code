    Atliq Mart Supply Chain Analysis

    Domain: FMCG

    Atliq Mart Supply Chain Analysis
    Project Details

    Designed a dashboard to measure the customer service level of AtliQ Mart ,who is a growing FMCG manufacturer headquartered in Gujarat,facing a problem, where few key customers experiencing supply chain issues.

    Project Overview

    This project focuses on analyzing the supply chain performance of AtliQ Mart, a growing FMCG company based in Gujarat, India. The company faced challenges with some key customers not renewing contracts due to service issues like delayed or incomplete deliveries. The objective was to track and improve service levels using three key metrics:

    • On-Time Delivery (OT%)
    • In-Full Delivery (IF%)
    • On-Time In-Full Delivery (OTIF%)

    Objective

    Develop a Power BI dashboard to monitor daily service performance, identify trends, and enable the supply chain team to respond proactively to issues. The analysis supports AtliQ Mart’s goal of resolving service challenges before expanding to Tier 1 cities.

    Key Features of the Dashboard

    1. KPI Performance Overview: Tracks OT%, IF%, and OTIF% against targets.
    2. Customer Analysis: Provides insights into customer-specific performance with visualizations.
    3. Product-Level Insights: Focuses on fill rate performance by product categories.
    4. Trend Analysis: Tracks KPIs over time, enabling management to pinpoint issues.
    5. Lead Time Analysis: Analyzes average delivery delays across cities and customers.

    Outcomes

    The project identified that KPI performance, particularly OT%, IF%, and OTIF%, was below target. It also highlighted product categories and customers requiring improvement, providing actionable insights to enhance supply chain efficiency.

    This project demonstrates expertise in supply chain analytics, Power BI dashboarding, and KPI tracking, showcasing the ability to deliver actionable business insights​.
